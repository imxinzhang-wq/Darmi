Trigger Identification

Avoid Triggers. Find Your Safe Foods.

IBS symptoms often show up hours — sometimes days — after the food that caused them, which is exactly why triggers are so hard to spot by memory alone. Darmi's pain analysis looks backward from each symptom entry and surfaces which foods appear most frequently in the window right before discomfort starts.

  • Automatic analysis of foods logged before symptom onset
  • Surfaces recurring patterns, not just single incidents
  • Helps distinguish likely trigger foods from your genuinely safe foods

Pattern Insights

Understand Your Body's Unique Patterns

Everyone's IBS is different — what triggers one person does nothing to another. Darmi's intelligent insights track how your symptoms trend over 7, 14, or 30-day windows, so you can see whether a change you made (a new food, less stress, a new routine) is actually working, not just guess.

  • Symptom trend tracking over 7 / 14 / 30-day periods
  • Key findings surfaced automatically — no spreadsheet required
  • Built to reveal your patterns, not generic IBS advice
