Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

01

Data Storage

  • All data (meals, symptoms, notes) is stored only on your device — mobile apps use the local database; browsers use IndexedDB.
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal health information to our servers.
  • Free and PRO users can store unlimited records on this device.
02

Data Export

  • Export your data as PDF for healthcare providers or personal records.
  • Free users: up to 1 export per calendar month (resets on the 1st). PRO: unlimited. Quota is tracked locally.
  • Exported files are created on your device; sharing them is under your control.
03

No Accounts, No Cloud Sync

  • No registration or login required.
  • No cloud backup or sync in this version.
  • Future versions may offer optional cloud sync with clear opt-in consent.
04

Third-Party Services

  • No advertising SDKs. May collect anonymous usage events (e.g. button taps, feature counts) to improve quality — never meal content, symptom details, or health text.
  • Emailed feedback may include basic technical info (app version, device, browser, locale, timestamp) for debugging, sent only if you email us.
05

Data Security

  • Protected by your device's security (lock screen, encryption).
  • App data is sandboxed from other sites and apps.
06

Your Rights

  • Nothing to delete on our side — we don't hold your data.
  • Delete all data anytime by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.
07

Children's Privacy

Darmi is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

08

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date above will reflect any changes. Significant updates will be communicated within the app.

09

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at darmi.feedback@gmail.com
